Frequently Asked Questions

Are bathroom cabinets with mirror worth it over a plain mirror?

Yes, if you are short on storage. A cabinet with a mirror gives you a place to look while hiding bottles, medications, and clutter behind the glass or doors, so you free up vanity and counter space. A plain mirror does neither.

How much should I spend on a bathroom cabinet with mirror?

Our picks run from $9.99 for a shower caddy set to $151.99 for a tall floor cabinet. For a freestanding unit with real storage, plan on $100 to $150. Our top value pick, the Joqixon, lands at $14.99 for renters who want more space without a big outlay.

What size bathroom cabinet fits a small bathroom?

Measure the gap before you buy. The TEENFON at 11.8 inches wide and the Iwell at 15.7 inches wide both fit narrow slots beside a toilet or vanity, while their 57.8 and 67.1 inch heights add storage by going up instead of out. For wider walls, the 22.8-inch Akxomel gives you more shelf room.

Do these cabinets need to be anchored to the wall?

Anchor the tall ones. The Iwell, HIFIT, and usikey all stand 67 inches or higher, and a tall, slim cabinet can tip if you load the top shelf. Each comes with hardware to secure it to the wall, and we recommend using it, especially in a home with kids.
